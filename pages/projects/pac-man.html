AI Pac-Man

AI Pac-Man is a class long project implementing search, decision making, and learning algorithms to develop an autonomous AI for Pac-Man, enabling self-play and strategic decision making.

Through a series of dynamic weekly iterations, I got to explore various topics of artificial intelligence with the famous arcade game Pac-Man. I delved into different search algorithms, implementing Depth First Search, Breadth First Search, and A* Search, to explore how Pac-Man's pathfinding behavior in a maze shifts in response to these search methods.

Building on this, I also learned how to build different types of reflex agents by incorporating decision-making algorithms such as minimax and expectimax search. With these decision-making methods, I got to witness how Pac-Man performed in different scenarios against ghosts.

Following this topic I continued to learning algorithms where I used the application of Markov Decision Processes to implement value iteration and Q-Learning to improve Pac-Man's performance against ghosts.
